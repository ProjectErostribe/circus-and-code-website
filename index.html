#CircusAndCode

A ♥ProjectErostribe♥ Thesis

Created by: PAIDAMOYO-JANET "My.L.E.S" AZEHKO

#CircusAndCode IS AN ATTEMPT TO SPARK TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION THROUGH THE EXPLORATION OF CIRCUS ARTS

#CircusAndCode is currently looking for Sponsors. Until then you can follow @CircusAndCode on Twitter and watch the livestreams for more information at: www.twitch.com/circusandcode



ACT 1 :

Build a DDR inspired game using JavaScript and Vue.js, #CircusAndCode music and a vintage DDR machine. Users can play in person, on the web and on your iOS or Android device. (Inspired by #100Devs Huntober)

ACT 2 :

Learn the art of Documentation using GitBook, GitHub CoPilot, Docusaurus, Loom and Twitch (Inspired by TessaK DevRel MasterMind // DocumentWrite - Portia Burton).

ACT 3 :

Build a backend for our game using JavaScript, Python and Ruby. Add to the music using Ruby Pi (Sponsored by Project Erostribe).

ACT 4 :

Design characters and build the online world. (Inspired by Disciple Media).

ACT 5 :

Mint an NFT using Solana, Solidity and Motoko. Use your GitHub participation to win NFTs, swag, and more. (RareBreed VC // Americana).

ACT 6 :

Design a sensory experience by foraging for food, and learn about QA by becoming a Quality Test Engineer. (Sponsored by Mandela CoOp).

ACT 7 :

Discover the effects of COVID-19 on the circus industry using Machine Learning and build a VR experience using Unity and C# (Juno Health // RareBreed VC).

ACT 8 :

Robotics and Wearables.

ACT 9 :

Bullet Stopping Drone (Astral AR - Leah la Salla).

ACT 10 :

Rigging and Construction Tech

ACT 11 :

Flying Motorcycle and Drones

ACT 12 :

Build your act and build technical skills at the #CircusAndCode retreat.

Compete for the chance to be part of #CircusAndCode LIVE! In Oakland, CA!
